How It Works

Three steps to a trained model

1. Profile & Detect

Automatically analyzes schema, missing values, distributions, and suggests target variables for prediction.

2. Preprocess & Train

Handles imputation, encoding, scaling, and trains multiple algorithms — Random Forest, SVM, KNN, and more.

3. Compare & Predict

Ranks models by performance metrics and provides a ready-to-use prediction interface with explanations.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

📊

Auto Data Profiling

Missing values, duplicates, data types, and statistics — all analyzed instantly after upload.

🧹

Smart Column Cleaning

Auto-drops useless columns (>80% missing, constants, ID-like), imputes and encodes the rest.
